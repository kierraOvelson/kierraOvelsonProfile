About

In April 2020 I will be graduating from the Web Design & Development stream in NAITs DMIT program. The DMIT course has given me the knowledge and ability to code and design functional and attractive websites. Many of the classes helped me to build a strong foundation in HTML and CSS; along with the basics in other coding languages, including: Javascript, PHP, and Wordpress. From a very young age I have been creating, whether it be building toys from scraps found in the recycling bin or drawing intricate artwork for my ongoing graphic novel. I thrive on finding solutions to design issues and with the training from NAIT, I have grown immensely in my abilities as both a designer and an individual.

I am always thinking creatively and eager to express my imagination through my designs. Nature and music play a large role in my imaginative thinking. Sometimes within a few short seconds I've come up with new ideas and can't sit still until I draw them out!
